How to Play

  1. Objective: The goal of the game is to answer as many quiz questions correctly as possible to earn points.
  2. Getting Started: Click the “Start” button to begin the quiz.
  3. Answering Questions: Each question will present four options. Click on the option you believe is correct.
  4. Scoring: You earn 1 point for each correct answer. Your current score will be displayed after each question.
  5. Navigation: After answering a question, the game will automatically move to the next question. If you reach the end of the quiz, your final score will be displayed.
  6. Ending the Game: Once all questions have been answered, you can choose to restart the quiz or view the leaderboard.
  7. Logging In: Make sure to log in to save your highest score. If you are logged in, the game will automatically save your score upon completion.
  8. Restarting and Leaderboard: You can restart the quiz or view your ranking after finishing.
  9. Logout: If you want to log out, click the “Logout” button. You will be redirected to the login page.
